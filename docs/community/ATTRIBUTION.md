# Attribution and Source Identity

Mobazha Community Edition was originally developed by
[fengzie](https://github.com/fengzie) and is maintained by the Mobazha
contributors.

The canonical source repository is
<https://github.com/mobazha/mobazha3.0>.

## Source-code notices

The unmodified Mozilla Public License 2.0 text is stored in the root `LICENSE`
file. Project identity, copyright ownership, inherited-code attribution, and
third-party scope are recorded in the root `NOTICE` file.

Distributors must preserve applicable license and copyright notices as required
by MPL-2.0 Section 3.4 and the licenses of incorporated material. Portions
derived from OpenBazaar remain subject to the MIT notice in
`LICENSES/MIT-OpenBazaar.txt`.

New first-party source files must include the following identifier near the top
of the file, using the language's normal comment syntax:

```text
SPDX-License-Identifier: MPL-2.0
```

Mobazha-originated files may additionally use this accurate notice:

```text
Copyright (c) 2021-2026 fengzie and the respective contributors.
```

Do not apply that copyright line to a third party's independent contribution
without permission. Contributors may add accurate notices for their own work.
Generated, vendored, and separately licensed files retain their own notices.

## Forks and branding

The MPL-2.0 source license permits forks and modification. It does not grant a
right to present a fork as an official Mobazha distribution. A distribution
that keeps the Mobazha name must follow `TRADEMARKS.md`, preserve the project
origin in `NOTICE`, and link to the canonical source. A rebranded fork may
remove Mobazha branding while continuing to preserve applicable source-code
license and copyright notices.

No user-interface attribution is imposed by the MPL-2.0 source license.
